Preparation

Before You Begin

Please ensure the following before starting your examination.

Use a Laptop

For the best examination experience, we recommend using a laptop or desktop computer. Mobile devices are supported but may provide a limited experience.

Stable Internet Connection

Ensure you have a reliable internet connection throughout your examination. Keeping a mobile hotspot as backup is recommended.

Keep Your Exam Code Ready

Your examination code will be shared by AIQM through email. Keep it ready before starting your examination.
